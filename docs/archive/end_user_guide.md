# End-User Guide: Creator Radar & Comment Copilot

Welcome to your AI-powered Action Desk. This guide explains how to populate your feed with target accounts, generate high-converting engagement strategies, and train the system on your unique tone.

## 1. Adding Creators to Radar
The **Creator Radar** is your command center for tracking industry leaders.
1. Open the web platform and navigate to the **Radar** page (`/radar`).
2. In the top right corner, paste a complete LinkedIn Profile URL (e.g., `https://www.linkedin.com/in/johndoe`).
3. Click **Add to Radar**. The system will register this target.
4. *Please allow a few moments for the background system to securely ingest their recent posts and populate your Action Desk Feed.*

## 2. Generating AI Strategies
Once posts appear in your feed, it's time to engage.
1. Scroll through your Action Desk Feed to review the original posts.
2. Below the post content, click the **Generate Comment Strategies** button.
3. The platform’s AI Engine will analyze the post context and ghostwrite 3 tailored strategies (e.g., Insightful, Contrarian, Supportive).

## 3. Copy & Track Feedback Loop
The most critical part of the platform is teaching the AI your authentic voice.
1. In the **AI Copilot Strategies** section, review the generated draft in the text editor box.
2. **Edit the text** to match your exact tone, add personal anecdotes, or fix any hallucinations. *Do not skip this step if the comment isn't perfect!*
3. Once satisfied, click the **Copy & Track Feedback** button.
4. **What happens under the hood?** The system copies the final text to your clipboard so you can tab over to LinkedIn and paste it. Simultaneously, the platform securely logs the original AI draft vs. your manual edits. 

> [!TIP]
> By actively editing strategies before copying them, you are directly fine-tuning the AI. Future comment generations will progressively adapt to sound more like your final edits!
