### How to use it — `http://localhost:3000/calendar`

1. Click the **🪄 AI Fill My Week** button (top-right of the calendar page)
2. A slide-in Drawer opens — fill in your **content pillars** (e.g. "AI Automation", "Founder Stories")
3. Set your **posts per week** (stepper: 1–7) and toggle **allowed formats** (Text, Carousel, Video)
4. Click **Generate N Posts** → Gemini 2.0 generates a full week of drafts
5. Posts auto-slot at your best heatmap times (Tue 10am, Thu 9am, etc.)
6. A **green preview strip** appears at the top of the calendar with each scheduled draft
